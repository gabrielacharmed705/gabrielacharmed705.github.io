## 🐛 Troubleshooting

* **File blocked by Windows:** Windows Defender might flag new software. Click the link provided in the warning to proceed with the installation.
* **Game crashes:** Ensure you use the current version of the game. If crashes persist, load your original save file.
* **Interface hangs:** Give the application a few seconds to scan your save files. This happens if you have many save slots.
* **Items missing:** Check that you selected Apply after choosing your items. Refresh your wardrobe in the game by sleeping or changing clothes at a tent.
